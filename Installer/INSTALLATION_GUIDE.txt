üîß POST-INSTALLATION SETUP:

1. Grant Accessibility Permissions (IMPORTANT!):
   - Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility
   - Find "Srz Clipboard" in the list
   - Enable the toggle switch
   - This enables automatic pasting functionality

2. Test the Installation:
   - Press Cmd+Alt+V to open clipboard history
   - Copy some text and try pasting
   - The app should appear in your menu bar
